This page lists all security reviews (“audits”) and certifications KeePassXC has received.

The reviews were conducted by independent organisations or individuals, and the KeePassXC Team had no influence on the results. The reviews are technical reports and do not constitute an endorsement of KeePassXC.

Note: Security reviews are bound to specific KeePassXC versions and platform builds. Nonetheless, the KeePassXC Team generally recommends using the latest version for the best user experience. The latest version can be found on the }}">Downloads page.
